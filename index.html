The Two Faces on Citi Bikes

Source: Citi Bike System Data

Subscriber

  • We pay $155/year or $14.95/month to get unlimited 45 miniute rides.
  • We get a unique key to unlock bicycles from the docking system.
  • We are people living in New York City.

Customer

  • We pay $9.95 for 24-Hours or $25 for 7-Day rides under 30 miniutes.
  • We use ride codes (from kiosks) to unlock the bicycles.
  • We are mostly tourists.
